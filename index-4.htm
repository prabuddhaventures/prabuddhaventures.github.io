SOLAR STREET LIGHTING SYSTEMS

Solar street Lighting offers a highly reliable solution which has high life system with a good visual quality.
Prabuddha Solar Street Lighting system includes Solar Photovoltaic module,
Long lasting deep duty cycle battery, pole , mounting structures and luminary with built
in LED driver and efficient solar charge controller for battery overcharge/deep discharge protection
and reverse polarity circuit damage protection.

Applications :

Area Lighting, Perimeter Security Lighting and Parking Lots of industrial, institutions and commercial areas, Parks, Private Roads , Jogging and Bike Path

Bus stops , rural roads, Remote Areas , Wildlife Areas , plantation areas

Roadways , Driveways, streets

Paths, Ramps in resorts, office complexes etc.

Fences , Gates, Docks , camp sites and beaches

Product Features/Benefits :

•  Highly reliable stand-alone system
•  Eliminate need for trenching and laying of cable
• Modular design: easy to assemble and maintenance, also saves    freight cost and installation cost

• All weather rugged pole with structure designed to operate at high    wind velocity
•  Maintenance free

•  Location flexibility

•  No utility bills and Unaffected by power Outages and Environmentally    friendly

SOLAR LANTERNS

Our Solar Powered Lantern is a portable light source like a traditional lantern which gives omni-directional pure white light. This solar powered lantern consists of a solar photovoltaic module, sealed maintenance free (SMF) lead acid battery, charge controller and DC-AC inverter for lighting the Compact Fluorescent Lamp (CF Lamp). We have two different grades suitable for two Prabuddha Jyot – Res
Prabuddha Jyot – Mil

The solar powered lantern manufactured by us is highly efficient. Its two-step charge controller protects battery from over charging and deep discharging. Our solar powered lantern is non-polluting and in exhaust energy from the Sun. Our superbly efficient solar powered lantern has positioned us in midst of innovative solar lantern manufacturers and portable solar lantern suppliers.

Roof Top Power-Packs
